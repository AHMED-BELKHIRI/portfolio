FACE TRACKER

Developed an advanced face tracking system, leveraging the MediaPipe library for robust face detection.
I engineered the software in Python and C, integrating a sophisticated motor control protocol on an STM32 microcontroller.
This setup enabled precise tracking movements, managed through local UART communication with a Raspberry Pi 4.
Additionally, I utilized SolidWorks for designing the mechanical components of the system, ensuring a seamless integration of electronics and hardware functionalities.

EUROBOT

I contributed to the design and development of two robots that met rigorous specification document requirements, focusing primarily on mechanical design.
A key achievement was engineering a 3-axis robotic arm controlled via an Arduino microcontroller.
